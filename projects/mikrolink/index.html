Monitor, manage, and control your MikroTik hotspot. Set up users, disable servers, update DNS records, or check traffic usage - all from your phone. Mikrolink makes managing your Mikrotik Hotspot easy, fast, and enjoyable.

About the App

Mikrolink is your ultimate tool for managing MikroTik RouterOS Hotspot. With a secure API-based connection, Mikrolink makes it easy to monitor router performance, manage hotspot users, control server status, and much more – all directly from your mobile device.

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Keep an eye on router performance and connected users in real time.
  • User Management: Create, edit, and delete hotspot users with intuitive tools and preset limits.
  • Traffic & Usage Analysis: Inspect usage, session status, traffic limits, MAC/IP addresses, and more.
  • DNS & Firewall Control: Easily manage DNS entries and firewall rules in one place.
  • Traffic Reports: Generate and export detailed traffic reports with a few taps (Premium feature).
  • Server & Interface Management: Control server status and network interfaces effortlessly.
  • Premium Access: Remove daily limits and unlock full features for an enhanced experience.

Designed specifically for MikroTik RouterOS Hotspot, Mikrolink eliminates the need for a laptop or Winbox with its clean, intuitive design and secure API-based connection. Enjoy fast, simple, and effective hotspot management on the go.
